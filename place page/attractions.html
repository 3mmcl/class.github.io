The city of Paris has an endless array of interesting, cultural wonders to experience. For the more casual admirers of the city, indulging in the unique cafe culture and marvelling at the Eiffel Tower which cuts into the sky from the cityscape can be a relaxing experience.

You can arrange a cruise on the large Seine river, wander the beautiful art at the Louvre, or venture across the city indulging in delicious foods by yourself or with the accompaniment of a friendly guide who can teach you all about the history and charm of the city.

For the more adventurous, diving into the Catacombs that expand beneath the city in a beguiling, tangled maze and learning the haunting, tragic, and solemn stories that creep in the shadows of the City of Light can be a thrill unlike any other.
